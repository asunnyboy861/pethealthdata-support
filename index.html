Frequently Asked Questions

How to add a new pet?

On the app's main interface, click the "Add Pet" button and fill in the pet's basic information.

How to record vaccinations?

Go to the pet's detail page, click on the "Vaccination Records" section, then click the "Add Vaccine" button and fill in the vaccine-related information.

How to export health reports?

On the pet's detail page, click the "Export Report" button, select the export format and time range, and the health report will be generated and exported.
